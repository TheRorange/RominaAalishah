Building efficient AI systems at Johns Hopkins University. I focus on making intelligent algorithms work on resource-constrained devices through hardware-aware optimization and novel architectures.

About

🎓 Education

PhD student in Electrical & Computer Engineering at Johns Hopkins University, working at the EEHPC Lab under Prof. Tinoosh Mohsenin.

⚡ Research Focus

Energy-efficient AI, edge computing, Mamba architectures, computer vision, and robotic navigation systems.

🌱 Approach

Combining strategic thinking with sustainable design to create AI systems that are both powerful and resource-efficient.

Research

Edge AI Optimization

Developing hardware-aware neural network compression techniques for efficient deployment on resource-constrained edge devices.

Mamba Architectures

Pioneering the use of Mamba models for image super-resolution, medical imaging, and object detection applications.

Robotic Navigation

Vision-language-action models using brain-inspired algorithms and reinforcement learning for autonomous navigation.

Knowledge Distillation

Transferring knowledge from large models to compact ones while maintaining performance for practical deployment.
